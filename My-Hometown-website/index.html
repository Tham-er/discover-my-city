Old Town

  • 🏰 Climb St. Olaf’s Church for panoramic city views
  • 🧱 Walk along the medieval city walls and towers
  • 🍽️ Enjoy Estonian cuisine at a traditional Old Town tavern
𝘅

Kalamaja

  • 🎨Explore the Telliskivi Creative City for street art and local design shops
  • 🐟Visit the Seaplane Harbour Museum for maritime history and real submarines
  • ☕Relax in cozy cafés inside colorful wooden houses
𝘅

Kadriorg

  • 🏛️ Stroll through Kadriorg Palace and its baroque gardens
  • 🖼️ Discover Estonian and international art at the Kumu Art Museum
  • 🌳 Take a peaceful walk or picnic in Kadriorg Park
